## ✅ THE PROBLEM (in one line)

Missing `REACT_APP_GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID` in `.env.local` file.

## ✅ THE SOLUTION (in one line)

Add `REACT_APP_GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID=YOUR_WEB_CLIENT_ID` to `.env.local` and restart server.

## ✅ THE RESULT

Google Sign-In works perfectly! ✨
